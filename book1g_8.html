"Things Fall Apart" by Chinua Achebe is a gripping tale set in Nigeria during the late 19th century. It follows Okonkwo, a respected warrior, as he grapples with the changes brought by colonialism. Through Okonkwo's story, Achebe explores themes of tradition, identity, and the collision of cultures. This timeless classic offers a vivid portrayal of Igbo society and the impact of colonialism on African communities.
