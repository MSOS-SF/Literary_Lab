Houghton Mifflin English is a comprehensive language arts curriculum designed for K-12 education, produced by Houghton Mifflin Harcourt. The program focuses on developing students' reading, writing, grammar, and communication skills through a variety of instructional materials, including textbooks, workbooks, and digital resources. The curriculum emphasizes a balanced approach to language learning, integrating literature, language skills, and writing instruction. It aims to foster critical thinking, creativity, and effective communication in students, providing them with the tools necessary for academic success and lifelong learning. Houghton Mifflin English is widely used in schools and classrooms across the United States and has been a trusted resource for educators seeking to enhance students' language proficiency.
